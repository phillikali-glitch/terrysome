Terrysome Investments Ltd — Uganda's Premier Licensed Labour Export and International Recruitment Agency

Terrysome Investments Ltd is a fully licensed labour export company and international manpower recruitment agency based in Kampala, Uganda. We specialize in sourcing, vetting, and deploying skilled and unskilled Ugandan workers to employers across the Middle East, Gulf Cooperation Council (GCC) countries, and beyond. Our services cover Saudi Arabia, United Arab Emirates (Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Sharjah), Qatar (Doha), Kuwait, Oman (Muscat), Bahrain, Jordan (Amman), Turkey, and select South American destinations.

Ugandan Workers Available for International Deployment

We recruit and deploy trade-tested, background-checked Ugandan professionals including: domestic workers, housemaids, house helpers, cleaners, security guards, professional drivers, caregivers, nurses, nannies, babysitters, construction workers, delivery personnel, and hospitality staff. All workers undergo Interpol clearance, medical screening, pre-departure orientation, and cultural training before deployment, ensuring they are visa-ready and employer-ready from day one.
